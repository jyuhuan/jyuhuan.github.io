Alignment

class Alignment(value)

Alignment in an axis.

A text block has two axes:

  • the x-axis goes from left to right horizontally, and

  • the y-axis goes from top to bottom vertically.

Use the values in the Alignment enum to choose a preferred general position (start, center, or end) in either the x-axis or the y-axis. The exact location is determined by the styled block that is asking for the alignment preference.
